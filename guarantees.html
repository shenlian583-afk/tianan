Framework

Core commitments included in qualified studio partnerships.

Non-Circumvention Commitment

We do not bypass studio partners to engage their end clients directly for the same project scope.

Role-Boundary Commitment

We execute engineering and manufacturing only. Concept design leadership remains with the studio.

IP and Data Protection

Project files follow NDA, need-to-know access, and controlled supplier communication protocols.

Commercial Transparency

Scope, payment milestones, and change-order triggers are documented with project-level traceability.

Prototype-to-Production Control

Tolerance, quality criteria, and critical characteristics are locked before pilot and carried into ramp.

Issue Escalation Governance

Defects and schedule risks follow a defined escalation path with owner accountability and closure timing.

Yes. These protections are standard requirements for studio-facing collaboration.
