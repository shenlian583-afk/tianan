Service Matrix

Scope and output by engagement model.

Prototype Innovation

Best for early-stage concepts that need architecture framing and rapid proof-of-feasibility.

Outputs: concept blueprint, feasibility report, prototype strategy, risk map.

Product Development

Best for validated concepts moving into engineering detail and production constraints.

Outputs: engineered CAD package, BOM strategy, DFM checkpoints, supplier shortlist.

Pilot Manufacturing

Best for teams preparing controlled launch runs and quality consistency.

Outputs: pilot SOP, QC framework, tolerance specs, assembly instructions.

Fractional Co-Dev

Best for ongoing innovation portfolios requiring strategic technical leadership.

Outputs: roadmap governance, cross-vendor integration, launch decision cadence.

Project Rhythm

The practical cadence we use to keep speed high without losing quality control.

Week 1

Intake Workshop + Success Criteria

Align stakeholders on user intent, business constraints, technical assumptions, and critical unknowns.

Week 2-3

Concept Structuring

Evaluate architecture options, materials strategy, and manufacturability risk before deep engineering spend.

Week 4-7

Engineering + Prototype Cycles

Run quick build-test loops to validate fit, function, and assembly logic against timeline and cost targets.

Week 8+

Pilot Transfer + Quality Gate

Lock production documentation and execute pilot readiness checks before scaling investment.
